Account & Data Deletion (Google Compliance)

We provide a clear path for you to delete your data:

To delete your account and all associated data:
1. Open the Profile section in Try Garage AI.
2. Copy your unique Device ID.
3. Email trygarages@gmail.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request" and include your ID.
4. We will purge your data from our systems within 30 days. Note: This action is permanent and deletes all Deco Coins.
