1. Privacy Policy

Try Garage AI operates a vehicle visualization platform. We prioritize your privacy and transparency.

Information We Collect

  • Pseudonymous IDs: Hashed Device Identifiers and Firebase UIDs.
  • Vehicle Photos: Processed in real-time for AI transformation; we do not store these permanently.
  • Advertising Data: Product info and images provided by advertisers.

Account & Data Deletion

You have the right to delete your data. To request deletion:
1. Go to your Profile in the app.
2. Copy your Device ID.
3. Send an email to trygarages@gmail.com with this ID.
Upon verification, all data associated with that ID will be purged within 30 days.
