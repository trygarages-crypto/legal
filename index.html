TRY GARAGE AI - PRIVACY POLICY

1. INTRODUCTION
Try Garage AI ('we', 'our', 'us') operates the Try Garage AI mobile application ('App'). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights. By using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

Contact: trygarages@gmail.com

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

2.1 Automatically Collected Data
- Hashed Device Identifier (SHA-256 one-way hash only)
- Firebase Anonymous Authentication UID
- Device model and OS version
- App version and Unity engine version
- First launch date (stored locally)
- Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) push token
- Anonymous usage events via Firebase Analytics

2.2 Information You Provide
- Image Capture: The App triggers the system's default camera application via Intent to allow users to capture vehicle photos. The App does NOT request direct access to the camera hardware (No CAMERA permission required).
- Photo Library Access: Users may optionally choose to upload vehicle photos from their device gallery.
- Real-time Processing: Vehicle photos submitted for AI transformation are processed in real-time. These photos are processed ephemerally and are NOT permanently stored on our servers.
- Support messages sent via your email client
- Advertising campaign data (product name, price, URL, image, campaign budget, duration, priority, bid score, analytics)
- Service tier classification data (automatically derived from your Deco Coin balance)
- Daily usage count for transformation limit enforcement (stored server-side, reset at midnight UTC)
- Image content flags generated by AI provider content moderation systems during transformation processing

2.3 Transaction Data
- Deco Coin balance and transaction history (stored in Firebase Realtime Database)
- Hashed purchase receipt identifiers
- Fraud detection logs where discrepancies are detected
- Service tier at time of each transaction
- Daily transformation count per user per UTC date (for usage limit enforcement)
- Account restriction status and restriction reason (stored in Firebase Realtime Database)

2.4 We Do NOT Collect
- Real name (unless voluntarily provided in support email)
- Precise location data
- Contacts, microphone, or biometric data
- Payment card details
- We do not sell personal data

3. HOW WE USE DATA
- Provide AI transformations
- Manage Deco Coin balances
- Process in-app purchases via Google Play
- Detect fraud and enforce Terms
- Deliver push notifications
- Improve app performance through analytics
- Operate and rank advertising campaigns fairly based on bid score
- Enforce service tier limits and daily usage quotas
- Detect and action prohibited image submissions
- Apply and record account suspensions or bans in accordance with our Terms of Service
